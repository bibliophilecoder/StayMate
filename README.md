# 🏡 StayMate

> **Find the right roommate, not just a room.**

StayMate is an AI-powered roommate compatibility platform designed to help students, interns, and working professionals find compatible flatmates based on lifestyle, personality, and living preferences rather than just location and budget.

Unlike traditional roommate platforms, StayMate focuses on reducing conflicts and improving the shared living experience through intelligent compatibility matching.

---

# 🎯 Problem Statement

Students and professionals relocating to a new city often struggle to find compatible roommates.

Existing roommate platforms primarily focus on location, rent, and availability while overlooking lifestyle compatibility, leading to conflicts, stress, and poor shared living experiences.

StayMate solves this problem by matching users based on personality, daily habits, and lifestyle preferences.

---

# 💡 Inspiration

The idea behind StayMate comes from the challenges faced by students and working professionals when relocating to unfamiliar cities. A compatible roommate can significantly improve mental well-being, comfort, productivity, and overall quality of life.
